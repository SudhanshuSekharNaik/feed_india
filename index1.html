Our Mission

Tackling Hunger: The Path of Anna Daan

In a country where vast amounts of food are wasted daily, our mission is to build a bridge of **'Anna Daan' (the sacred act of donating food)**. We connect surplus food from restaurants and events with local NGOs and shelters, ensuring that no meal is wasted while so many go hungry. Our goal is to transform food waste into a vital resource, making a direct impact on communities in need.

Building a Community of Compassion

We are a passionate team dedicated to creating a transparent and efficient food donation network. Our platform connects individuals and businesses with those who need it most, helping to nourish India one meal at a time. This is more than a platform; it's a movement to create a sustainable ecosystem of giving, fueled by compassion and a belief that access to food is a fundamental right.

Key Features

User Dashboards

A dedicated space for donors to track their impact and for NGOs to manage requests, volunteers, and distribution efficiently.

Seamless Communication

Integrated messaging and real-time alerts ensure a smooth, secure, and reliable connection between donors and recipients.

Food Safety & Trust

A robust system for donor vetting, food preparation guidelines, and a rating system to build trust and ensure safety for all.

Live Tracking

Real-time tracking of food pickup and delivery with integrated maps.

QR Code Scanning

Quick food logging and verification using QR code technology.

Gamification

Earn points, badges, and rewards for your contributions.
