Our Mission

Tackling Hunger: The Path of Anna Daan

In a country where vast amounts of food are wasted daily, our mission is to build a bridge of Anna Daan (the sacred act of donating food). We connect surplus food from restaurants and events with local NGOs and shelters, ensuring that no meal is wasted while so many go hungry.

Building a Community of Compassion

We are a passionate team dedicated to creating a transparent and efficient food donation network. Our platform connects individuals and businesses with those who need it most, helping to nourish India one meal at a time.
